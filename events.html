Kosmic Noggings

Aug 2024

An Astronomy Quiz centered around space science and eminent scientists was conducted on 23rd August, marking National Space Day—the historic day of Chandrayaan-3’s successful lunar landing. Organized in collaboration with Avasya, the quiz celebrated India’s achievement in space exploration while testing participants on astronomy, space missions, and the contributions of renowned scientists.
